## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Verify multi-threading across 6-10 decision makers.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Email** CC lists
- **Zoom** participant transcripts
- **LinkedIn**

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Analyze** communication patterns to identify the full buying committee (6-10 people).
2. **Categorize** stakeholders into **Executive**, **Functional**, **Operational**, and **IT** roles.
3. **Ensure** the **CS** team is "multi-threaded" to prevent single-point-of-failure risk.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** all identified stakeholders are verified against the customer's official organization chart.
- **DO NOT** store sensitive personal information about stakeholders without their consent.
