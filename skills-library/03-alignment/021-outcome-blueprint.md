## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Align vendor and client on a long-term strategic roadmap.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Milestone** data
- **Forward-looking** strategic goals

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Review** current milestone attainment against the long-term strategic goals.
2. **Update** the **Outcome Blueprint** to reflect changing business priorities.
3. **Facilitate** a strategic alignment session to ensure the product remains indispensable.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** strategic goals to be reviewed and updated at least once per quarter.
- **ENSURE** the **Outcome Blueprint** is a collaborative document that is shared with the customer.
