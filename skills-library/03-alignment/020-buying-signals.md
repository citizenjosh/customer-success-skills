## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Identify high-intent expansion opportunities.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Website** visit patterns
- **LinkedIn** engagement data

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Monitor** for external buying signals, such as visits to pricing pages or **LinkedIn** engagement.
2. **Correlate** these signals with internal account health and usage trends.
3. **Alert** the revenue team to high-intent expansion opportunities in real-time.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** external signals to be correlated with internal usage data to ensure they are high-intent.
- **DO NOT** alert the revenue team unless the signal is consistently linked to expansion.
