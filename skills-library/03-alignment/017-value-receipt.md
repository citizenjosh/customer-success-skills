## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Provide **CFO**-approved evidence of software financial impact.

## DATA INPUTS

- **ROI** benchmark data
- **VRE** performance logs

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Compare** realized product performance against the customer's **ROI** benchmarks.
2. **Generate** an automated "**Value Receipt**" that quantifies **NRR** impact and **OPEX** reduction.
3. **Deliver** the receipt to the **CFO** to secure renewal and expansion budgets.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** all financial impact data to be verified by the customer's internal finance team.
- **ENSURE** the **Value Receipt** is delivered to the **CFO** in a format that is easy to understand and act upon.
