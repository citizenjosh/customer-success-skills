## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Reduce manual reporting work for **CSMs** by 80%.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Product** usage trends
- **Support** history
- **CRM** data

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Aggregate** product usage, support, and health data into a standardized report.
2. **Use** **AI** to draft summaries and identify trend patterns for the review session.
3. **Automate** the generation of **QBR** decks, freeing the **CSM** for strategic advisory.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** all automated summaries to be reviewed and edited by the **CSM** to ensure they are accurate and professional.
- **ENSURE** the **QBR** deck follows the customer's preferred branding and format.
