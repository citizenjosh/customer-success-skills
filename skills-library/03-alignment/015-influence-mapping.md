## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Target engagement strategy for high-power "Economic Buyers".

## DATA INPUTS

- **Stakeholder** interaction frequency
- **Sentiment** analysis

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Map** the influence and power dynamics within the customer's organization.
2. **Identify** the "**Economic Buyer**" (**CFO**/**CXO**) and their primary business interests.
3. **Align** engagement efforts to ensure high-power stakeholders see quantifiable **ROI**.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** influence maps to be treated as highly confidential and only shared with the internal account team.
- **ENSURE** mapping is based on objective interaction data, **NOT** subjective opinions.
