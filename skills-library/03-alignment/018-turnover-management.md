## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Rapidly re-align with new champions to prevent churn.

## DATA INPUTS

- **LinkedIn** profile changes
- **"Out of Office"** auto-replies

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Monitor** for stakeholder turnover signals (e.g., **LinkedIn** updates or **OOO** replies).
2. **Identify** the new champion or decision-maker immediately upon their arrival.
3. **Initiate** a "Re-onboarding" sequence to align the new stakeholder with the **Outcome Blueprint**.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** re-onboarding sequences to be tailored to the new stakeholder's specific role and interests.
- **ENSURE** the turnover alert is triggered immediately to prevent any loss of momentum.
