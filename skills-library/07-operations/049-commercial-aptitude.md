## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Transition the **CS** team into a **Revenue Engineering** function.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Performance** reviews
- **P&L** fluency tests

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Assess** the **CS** team's commercial aptitude and **P&L** fluency.
2. **Provide** training on revenue engineering and financial **ROI** modeling.
3. **Align** **CS** performance metrics with core financial outcomes (**NRR**/**ARR**).

## GUARDRAILS

- **TRAINING** should be focused on the financial impact of Customer Success, not just sales techniques.
- **ENSURE** performance metrics include a balance of customer satisfaction and financial outcomes.
- **ENSURE** revenue-based performance metrics are weighted by 'Customer Health' scores to prevent predatory renewal practices.
- **ESCALATE** to Human CSM if sentiment analysis score drops below 0.3.
