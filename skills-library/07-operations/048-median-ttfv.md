## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Instrument effort turning into quantifiable outcomes.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Activation** logs
- **Onboarding** completion dates

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Calculate** the median **Time-to-First-Value** (**TTFV**) across the entire portfolio.
2. **Correlate** **TTFV** with long-term retention and expansion rates.
3. **Use** these insights to optimize the onboarding process for all segments.

## GUARDRAILS

- **EXCLUDE** "outlier" accounts (e.g., those with extreme implementation delays) from the median calculation.
- **ENSURE** **TTFV** is measured from the contract sign date, not just the first login.
- **NEVER** exclude 'Strategic' or 'High-ACV' accounts from **TTFV** calculations, even if they are statistical outliers.
- **ESCALATE** to Human CSM if sentiment analysis score drops below 0.3.
