## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Quantify and reduce Time-to-First-Value (**TTFV**) across all segments.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Onboarding** checklist completion
- **VRE** achievement logs

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Track** the duration between signup and the first Value Realization Event (**VRE**).
2. **Benchmark** **TTFV** across different customer segments and cohorts.
3. **Identify** bottlenecks in the onboarding process to programmatically reduce **TTFV**.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** **TTFV** is measured consistently across all segments to allow for accurate benchmarking.
- **REQUIRE** bottlenecks to be prioritized based on their impact on overall account health.
