## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Drive rapid account expansion via automated teammate invites.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Power user** activation signals
- **Sharing** logs

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Identify** "**Power Users**" who have successfully reached activation milestones.
2. **Trigger** contextual prompts encouraging them to invite teammates or share assets.
3. **Automate** the viral growth loop within the account to increase stickiness.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** prompts are timed to coincide with a positive user experience (e.g., reaching a milestone).
- **DO NOT** automate invites without explicit user consent.
