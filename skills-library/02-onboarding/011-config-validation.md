## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Ensure new accounts are correctly configured for maximum value realization.

## DATA INPUTS

- **System** config logs
- **BEST-practice** benchmarks

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Scan** new account configurations against established best-practice benchmarks.
2. **Identify** misconfigurations that could delay time-to-first-value.
3. **Autonomously** validate or suggest fixes to ensure the account is optimized for **ROI**.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** benchmarks to be updated regularly to reflect the latest product best practices.
- **ENSURE** all suggested fixes are reviewed and approved by a human **CSM** for high-value accounts.
