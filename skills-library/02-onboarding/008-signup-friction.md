## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Maxmize signup completion with minimal abandonment.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Funnel** conversion logs
- **Form** field completion rates

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Audit** the signup funnel to identify fields or steps with high abandonment rates.
2. **Implement** progressive disclosure to minimize initial cognitive load.
3. **Optimize** the flow to ensure signup completion with fewer than 3 required fields.

## GUARDRAILS

- **REQUIRE** any changes to the signup flow to be **A/B** tested to ensure they do not negatively impact conversion.
- **ENSURE** all **REQUIRED** fields are essential for the user's initial success.
- **DO NOT** remove or delay security-critical fields (MFA, ToS) to reduce friction.
