## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Codify VRE targets based on specific client ROI goals.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Sales-to-CS** handoff notes
- **CRM** deal context

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Extract** business outcomes and success metrics defined during the sales cycle.
2. **Map** these outcomes to specific Value Realization Events (**VREs**) in the product.
3. **Establish** a baseline for measuring **ROI** that aligns with the customer's financial goals.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** all extracted outcomes are validated with the customer during the first onboarding call.
- **DO NOT** map outcomes that are not directly supported by current product features.
- **REQUIRE** the agent to flag outcomes that exceed historical performance benchmarks for human review.
