## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Fuel new logo acquisition via satisfied power users.

## DATA INPUTS

- **High-NPS** sentiment
- **VRE** success stories

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Identify** customers with high **NPS** and verified **VRE** success stories.
2. **Incentivize** these advocates to provide referrals or participate in case studies.
3. **Leverage** Customer Success to increase the fiscal relationship with an existing customer by pleasing the end-users and offering discernible value.

## GUARDRAILS

- **DO NOT** ask for referrals from customers with open high-priority support tickets.
- **ENSURE** the incentive offered is compliant with the customer's corporate gift policies.
- **ENSURE** **NPS**/Sentiment data is less than 30 days old and check for recent 'Critical' support tickets before asking for referrals.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
