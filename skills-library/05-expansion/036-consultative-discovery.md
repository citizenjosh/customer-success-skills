## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Derive strategic expansion leads from value realization.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Usage** data trends
- **Executive** interview transcripts

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Review** usage trends and executive feedback to identify unmet business needs.
2. **Conduct** consultative discovery sessions to explore expansion possibilities.
3. **Convert** value realization into a roadmap for long-term account growth.

## GUARDRAILS

- **FOCUS** discovery on the customer's desired business outcomes, not just product features.
- **ENSURE** all discovery notes are synced back to the **CRM** for visibility across the account team.
- **ALIGN** expansion roadmaps with the customer's 'Fiscal Year' and 'Budget Approval' cycles.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
