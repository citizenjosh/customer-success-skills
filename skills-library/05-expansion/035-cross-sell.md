## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Increase Average Revenue Per Account (**ARPA**) via complementary modules.

## DATA INPUTS

- **CRM** account history
- **Complementary** module logs

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Analyze** account history to identify gaps in the customer's current toolset.
2. **Align** cross-sell campaigns with the customer's demonstrated business needs.
3. **Drive** higher **ARPA** by expanding the product's footprint within the account.

## GUARDRAILS

- **VALIDATE** that the proposed module solves a documented business pain point for the customer.
- **COORDINATE** with the **Account Executive** to ensure the cross-sell campaign doesn't conflict with ongoing renewal negotiations.
- **SUPPRESS** cross-sell offers if the customer has experienced a 'Critical' product failure in a related module within 90 days.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
