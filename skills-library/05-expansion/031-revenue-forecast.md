## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Provide high-accuracy pipeline predictability for board-level reviews.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Historical** churn
- **Usage** patterns
- **Market** trends

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Analyze** historical usage and churn patterns to forecast expansion revenue.
2. **Incorporate** market trends and competitive signals into the predictive model.
3. **Deliver** a high-confidence revenue forecast for executive and board reviews.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ALWAYS** include a confidence interval or margin of error with every forecast.
- **DO NOT** rely solely on historical data; **INCORPORATE** current pipeline velocity and market volatility.
- **INCORPORATE** 'Regulatory Risk' and 'Macroeconomic Headwinds' as explicit discount factors in the forecast.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
