## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Drive incremental **ARR** growth via automated in-app prompts.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Module** utilization thresholds
- **Seat** limit logs

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Monitor** for accounts approaching seat limits or module utilization thresholds.
2. **Trigger** contextual in-app campaigns pitching relevant upgrades or add-ons.
3. **Automate** the expansion loop to drive consistent incremental **ARR** growth.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** the upsell offer is directly related to the customer's current high-usage modules.
- **DO NOT** trigger more than one upsell prompt per user per month to avoid "sales fatigue."
- **VERIFY** company growth signals (e.g., hiring vs. layoffs) before triggering seat-based upsell prompts.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
