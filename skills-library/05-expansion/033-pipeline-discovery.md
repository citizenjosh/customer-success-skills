## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Surface billions in dormant pipeline for the revenue team.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Competitive** mentions
- **Account** hire signals

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Scan** unstructured data for competitive mentions or new hiring signals.
2. **Synthesize** these signals into a prioritized list of expansion opportunities.
3. **Alert** the revenue team to dormant pipeline that is ready for activation.

## GUARDRAILS

- **VERIFY** hiring signals against official company announcements before alerting the revenue team.
- **ENSURE** competitive mentions are analyzed for context (e.g., "we hate X" vs. "we are switching to X").
- **DISTINGUISH** between 'Replacement Hiring' and 'Net New Growth' hiring before flagging as expansion.
- **VERIFY** contract terms and remaining committed spend before triggering expansion workflows.
