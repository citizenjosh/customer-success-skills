## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Identify "**Value Gaps**" before they lead to churn.

## DATA INPUTS

- **VRE** targets vs. actual product telemetry

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Audit** the gap between the customer's **VRE** targets and their actual feature usage.
2. **Identify** core **ROI**-driving features that remain underutilized.
3. **Trigger** targeted adoption campaigns to bridge the identified "**Value Gap**."

## GUARDRAILS

- **NEVER** recommend a feature without verifying the customer's current license tier.
- **ENSURE** the "**Value Gap**" is quantified in financial or time-saved terms before alerting the **CSM**.
- **VERIFY** 'grandfathered' status and specific contract addendums before recommending upgrades.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
