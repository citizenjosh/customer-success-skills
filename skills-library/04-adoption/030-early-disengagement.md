## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Trigger proactive intervention based on behavioral "dots".

## DATA INPUTS

- **Subtle** usage log changes
- **Login** frequency drops

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Monitor** for subtle drops in login frequency or feature usage patterns.
2. **Identify** these "dots" as early signals of customer disengagement.
3. **Alert** the **CSM** to initiate a proactive health check before the risk escalates.

## GUARDRAILS

- **DISTINGUISH** between seasonal usage drops and genuine disengagement signals.
- **ENSURE** the proactive health check is framed as a value-add, not a "why aren't you using us" interrogation.
- **CORRELATE** usage drops with 'Project Completion' milestones before flagging as disengagement.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
