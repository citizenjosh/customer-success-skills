## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Establish a repeatable path for customers to reach "Expert" adoption.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Adoption level assessments**
- **Playbook** usage

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Assess** the customer's current adoption maturity against industry benchmarks.
2. **Deploy** specific playbooks from the "**Maturity Rolodex**" to drive advanced usage.
3. **Guide** the customer through a structured path toward "Expert" status.

## GUARDRAILS

- **DO NOT** skip maturity levels; **ENSURE** foundational features are adopted before advanced playbooks.
- **VALIDATE** that the customer's stated goals align with the "Expert" path being proposed.
- **VALIDATE** user proficiency via qualitative feedback or certification status before advancing maturity levels.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
