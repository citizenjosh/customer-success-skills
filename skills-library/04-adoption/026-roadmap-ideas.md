## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Create a direct pipeline of verified customer needs into **R&D**.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Transcript NLP**
- **Sentiment analysis** of requests

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Use** **NLP** to extract product requests and pain points from customer conversations.
2. **Analyze** the sentiment and frequency of these requests to prioritize roadmap items.
3. **Deliver** a verified list of customer needs directly to the **R&D** team.

## GUARDRAILS

- **AGGREGATE** similar requests to avoid overwhelming **R&D** with duplicate items.
- **PRIORITIZE** requests from high-value or high-risk accounts to maximize impact.
- **ENSURE** all prioritized roadmap items undergo a 'Security & Compliance Impact' review before delivery to **R&D**.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
