## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Initiate save plays for high-sentiment/low-usage accounts.

## DATA INPUTS

- **NPS** data vs. feature utilization telemetry

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Identify** "**Zombie Accounts**" with high **NPS** but low feature utilization.
2. **Trigger** a re-onboarding campaign to re-engage these accounts with value.
3. **Monitor** for a shift toward **Value Realization Events** to prevent sudden churn.

## GUARDRAILS

- **DO NOT** trigger re-onboarding for accounts that have explicitly opted out of marketing communications.
- **VERIFY** that "low usage" isn't due to a known system outage or holiday period.
- **CHECK** for 'Strategic Hold' or 'Quiet Period' flags in **CRM** before triggering automated re-engagement.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
