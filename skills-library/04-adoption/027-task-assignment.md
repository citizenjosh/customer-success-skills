## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Ensure 100% accountability for post-meeting follow-up.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Summarized** call action items
- **CRM** contact roles

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Extract** action items and tasks from meeting summaries automatically.
2. **Assign** these tasks to the appropriate owners based on **CRM** roles.
3. **Track** task completion to ensure no customer request is overlooked.

## GUARDRAILS

- **VERIFY** owner availability before assigning urgent tasks.
- **ENSURE** all tasks have a clear deadline and a link back to the original customer request.
- **SYNC** with **HRIS** or **OOO** calendars to ensure 'Availability' accounts for leave, not just calendar gaps.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
