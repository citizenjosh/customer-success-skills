## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Continuously refine lifecycle messaging and strategy.

## DATA INPUTS

- **CRM** historical deal data
- **Churn** reason codes

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Conduct** monthly reviews of won, lost, and churned accounts.
2. **Identify** recurring themes in churn reason codes and deal losses.
3. **Update** the **CS** lifecycle strategy to address identified weaknesses.

## GUARDRAILS

- **ENSURE** win/loss reviews include feedback from both the customer and the internal account team.
- **USE** the findings to update the "**Ideal Customer Profile**" (**ICP**) to avoid future bad-fit churn.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
