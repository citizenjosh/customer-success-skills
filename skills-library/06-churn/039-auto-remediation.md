## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Resolve technical and contractual hurdles autonomously via **AI**.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Churn** risk alert
- **CRM**/**Billing** system status

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Intercept** churn risk alerts triggered by predictive models.
2. **Initiate** autonomous remediation playbooks (e.g., contract adjustments).
3. **Resolve** hurdles without human intervention to protect recurring revenue.

## GUARDRAILS

- **NEVER** apply a contract adjustment that exceeds a 10% discount without human approval.
- **ENSURE** all autonomous actions are logged in the **CRM** with a clear "**Auto-Remediated**" tag.
- **VERIFY** 'Churn Intent' via sentiment or direct feedback before applying autonomous financial remediation.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
