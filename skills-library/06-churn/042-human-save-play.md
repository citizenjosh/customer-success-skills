## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Preserve high-value accounts through strategic human touch.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Predictive** churn alert
- **High-ACV** account signal

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Filter** predictive churn alerts for high-**ACV** or strategic accounts.
2. **Alert** the **CSM** to initiate a high-touch "**Human Save Play**."
3. **Use** strategic advisory to resolve complex value or relationship issues.

## GUARDRAILS

- **PRIORITIZE** accounts with the highest **ACV** and strategic importance for immediate human outreach.
- **ENSURE** the **CSM** has access to a full "**Risk Summary**" before contacting the customer.
- **ROUTE** '**Human Save Plays**' based on **CSM** capacity and expertise, escalating to leadership if all **CSMs** are at capacity.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
