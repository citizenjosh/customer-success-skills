## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Rapidly resolve technical crises via autonomous agents.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Complaint** sentiment
- **Ticket** priority
- **Account** history

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Identify** high-priority crisis tickets using sentiment analysis.
2. **Deploy** autonomous agents to provide immediate technical de-escalation.
3. **Stabilize** the account while escalating to human experts for final resolution.

## GUARDRAILS

- **AUTONOMOUS AGENTS** must **NEVER** admit legal liability or offer financial compensation.
- **ENSURE** a human expert is looped in within 15 minutes of a "**High Priority**" crisis detection.
- **ENSURE** all autonomous 'workarounds' are pre-validated for security integrity and do not bypass existing controls.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
