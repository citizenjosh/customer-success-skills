## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Mitigate competitive replacement threats during **CFO** audits.

## DATA INPUTS

- **Client** utilization of overlapping toolsets
- **Competitive** feature mapping

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Audit** the customer's tech stack for overlapping or redundant tools.
2. **Demonstrate** the superior value and integration of your product.
3. **Secure** your position in the stack before the **CFO** initiates consolidation.

## GUARDRAILS

- **FOCUS** on the unique value and deep integration of your product, not just feature parity.
- **IDENTIFY** the key **CFO**-level metrics (e.g., **ROI**, **TCO**) that will drive the consolidation decision.
- **VERIFY** 'Customer-Specific Compliance Policies' or 'Banned Feature' lists before highlighting features for consolidation.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
