## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Strategically redistribute budget to stable customer segments.

## DATA INPUTS

- **GRR**/**NRR** data by vertical
- **ACV** segmentation

## EXECUTION LOGIC

1. **Benchmark** **GRR** and **NRR** performance across different verticals and segments.
2. **Identify** segments with high stability and growth potential.
3. **Reallocate** **CS** resources and budget to maximize overall portfolio health.

## GUARDRAILS

- **DO NOT** reallocate budget away from a segment without a 3-month trend analysis.
- **ENSURE** vertical-specific nuances (e.g., seasonal churn) are accounted for in the benchmarking.
- **RESPECT** user communication preferences and opt-out statuses.
- **VERIFY** data trends across a 30-day window to avoid knee-jerk reactions.
